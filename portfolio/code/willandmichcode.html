Purpose

The purpose of this website is that my wife and I needed a way to collect RSVP from the people that we have invited for our wedding. Instead of spending extras and creating RSVP cards with return addressed, we decided to do it online to save a lot of time. It not only saves us money but eventually if we do a printed RSVP card we still need to put it in a spreadsheet to see who is coming or not. So I hooked a Google Form in there with the RSVP results sending an email to us and populating a Google spreadsheet.
