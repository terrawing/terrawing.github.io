Let's get to know about me!

Home > About

I am currently a freelance designer with many job titles and have done work for graphic design both print as well as digital. I have worked on user interface designs for all ages as a front end developer and developed websites with effective designs around the limitations of the web. In addition to development, I have an understanding of user experience design that provides meaningful, relevant experiences to users of both new and existing products.

Growing up, design was not my first choice in career. Design was an interest that had actually grown over the years leading to this current day. Working in this career path is something I find great interest in as I look to see how I can see the world in a better creative perspective.

In all my projects whether they are for user experience, interfaces or coding, I often try to understand what is the actual issue that clients do not see in the forefront. For example, sometimes a client would say they need a new design all together, but in actuality their best approach is to take what they have now and elaborate the strengths of it instead. I would say my expertise in these scenarios are consulting and it is something I have been doing in both freelance work and my past jobs.

I personally enjoy working on projects that are completely new that requires a preliminary researching phase all the way into the end towards the visual design. I love to start from the beginning because it allows me to create products that are more inline with the user since I have a better understanding of the user's requirements or needs and it allows me to make adjustments if those requirements or needs change during the process.

Besides working on projects, I am always trying to grow and learn something new. This can be new programming libraries, new design trends or even new personal hobbies. Currently I am looking for new opportunities and am open to new projects.
