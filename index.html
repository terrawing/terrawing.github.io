I am a designer from the heart of Toronto that started doing graphic design since September 2005 and computer programming in January 2011. I have currently graduated from Seneca College majoring in Bachelor of Software Development.

I have designed various promotional materials for many companies to advertise their products. During my time in school, I also have gained experience in both front and back end web development. Recently, I have acquired knowledge on both User Experience designs and human factors. With these I am able to create personas, conduct human computer interaction research and create low and high fidelity sketches.
